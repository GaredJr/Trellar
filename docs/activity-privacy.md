# Activity Privacy

## Purpose

The activity feed helps workspace members understand recent board and card changes.

## Visibility

- Feed visibility is limited to workspace members.
- Exported logs must follow your organization's retention and access rules.

## Data Included

- Action title
- Board/workspace source
- Timestamp

## Best Practices

- Avoid adding sensitive personal data in card titles.
- Review exported logs before sharing externally.
- Apply least-privilege access for team roles.
